Poached Sweet Pea

back

Description

Basically just your simple poached Sweet Pea recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Sweet Pea
  • One bath tub full of water
  • bubblebath liquid, 1 cup
  • one wash cloth

Steps

  1. Heat water in tub to approx 100deg F
  2. Add peeled Sweet Pea to water
  3. lather with bubblebath
  4. allow to soak for about 10 minutes
  5. remove sweet pea from water and dry off
  6. dress up sweet pea as needed
