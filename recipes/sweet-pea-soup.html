Description

This recipe was inspired by the power puff girls. It's how i imagine a sweet pea is made

Ingredients

  • Sugar, 1 dash
  • Spice, 1 sprinkle
  • Everything Nice 1, 1/4tsp
  • Chemical X, 3 gallons

Steps

  1. Mix sugar, spice, and everything nice
  2. Add Chemical X
  3. Avoid Explosion
  4. Add additional Chemical X to taste
