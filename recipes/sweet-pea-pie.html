Sweet Pea Pie

back

Description

When making a sweet pea pie, never skimp on the sweetness

Ingredients

  • sweetness, 8 cups
  • cuteness, 3 cups
  • sassyness, 5 cups
  • hair, 1000 pc

Steps

  1. mix sweetness and cuteness, being carful not to get overwhelmed
  2. simmer sweet-cute mixture on low for 15 minutes
  3. "Fold-in" the sasiness and allow the mixture to cool down
  4. apply hair liberally
